Frequently Asked Any Questions

1.Where do you source your fresh food products from?

We source our fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy, and other products directly from local farmers and trusted organic suppliers. Our priority is to provide high-quality, chemical-free, and farm-fresh ingredients to our customers.

2.Do you offer home delivery, and how long does it take?

Yes, we offer fast and reliable home delivery! Depending on your location, orders are typically delivered within 24 hours. For same-day delivery, please place your order.

3.How do you ensure the freshness of your products?

We follow strict quality control measures, including cold storage, direct farm-to-store supply, and careful packaging to maintain freshness. We also recommend consuming perishable items within the recommended time for the best taste and quality.
